fenn / crossbar — concept variants

Five ways the crossbar can behave.

Same wordmark, same font (Outfit Light 300), same dark green ground (#0d2018), same cream marks (#fafaf7). Only the crossbar changes — its extent, its breaks, its asymmetry — so you can judge the device, not the typeface.

bridges F to E · contained, no extensions
02

Extends past both ends

bar becomes a brand element · runs past the wordmark on both sides
03

Replaces F and E's horizontal stroke entirely

heavy bar at stroke weight · F and E share one bar as a single unified element
04

Broken into segments

three short bars between F-E, E-N, N-N · creates rhythm
05

Asymmetric horizon

long extension on the left · ends inside the wordmark · intentionally lopsided
